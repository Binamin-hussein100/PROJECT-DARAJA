About Us

Inefficient communication means within the slum is a common problem

Opportunities arise on a daily basis, as a result of the inefficient communication channels, not everyone gets to hear about these opportutnities. In most cases, the opportuities are sold to the highest bidder.

We as project daraja aim to level the playing field by ensuring everyone gets to hear about anay available opportuity and giving them a chance to apply

Our project name is Project Daraja and our motto; Ideal gorvernance

Due to lack of proper communication channel, many opportunities meant for the youths are lost to corruption and nepotism. The people who deliver the news choose to sell the opportunities or give it to people close to them. Project Daraja aims to develop a system that will be accessible by both the youths and constituency administration . The system will allow the administration to post opportunities to the general population and also allows the youth to send direct messages to the administration.
